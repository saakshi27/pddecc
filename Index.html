ABOUT US

Principal Directorate Defence Estates is the HQrs of Defence Estates Service in Central Command. Principal Director, Defence Estates is the head of the Directorate. Principal Director, Defence Estates provides advisory inputs upon all Cantonments (in Central Command) and land matters to Army HQRs Central Command and other organizations under the purview of MoD. Principal Director, Defence Estates also ensures implementation of Cantonments Act 2006, Policies, Rules & Regulations as also Executive Instructions issued by Higher authorities from time to time.

Principal Director, Defence Estates, Central Command has under its jurisdiction offices of Nine Defence Estates Officers and Twenty Five Chief Executive Officers spread in Five States of the Country for management of Defence Lands/Cantonments in the respective areas of their jurisdiction. There are Twenty Five Cantonments in Central Command and it is their responsibility to provide Civic amenities to the local residents of Cantonment Area as also to implement Central Government Schemes of Social Welfare.

Name of the Cantonments under its jurisdiction are:-

Agra, Allahabad, Almora, Bareilly, Chakrata, Clement Town, Danapur, Dehradun, Faizabad, Fatehgarh, Jabalpur, Kanpur, Landour, Lansdowne, Lucknow, Mathura, Meerut, Mhow, Nainital, Pachmarhi, Ranikhet, Ramgarh, Roorkee, Shahjahanpur and Varanasi.

Name of the DEO circles under its jurisdiction are:-

Agra, Allahabad, Bareilly, Bhubaneshwar, Danapur, Jabalpur, Lucknow, Meerut and Mhow.
